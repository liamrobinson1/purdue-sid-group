Brandon Castillo
================

Brandon graduated from Virgina Tech with a BS in Aerospace & Ocean Engineering in 2020 and spent 2 years spent working as a software and systems engineer at Peraton before returning for a graduate degree. He is pursuing an MS with Dr. Frueh, looking at recommended end-of-life maneuvers or procedures for objects in cislunar space. Outside of his studies he practices kickboxing/MMA, enjoys writing music, and is getting back into rock climbing.
