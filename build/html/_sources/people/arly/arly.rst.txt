Arly Black
==========

Arly is currently pursuing her PhD degree with Dr. Frueh in the Space Information Dynamics group and hopes to graduate sometime this decade. Her research focuses on characterizing breakup events in the cislunar regime and forensic analysis of near-Earth fragmentation events. She obtained her BS in mechanical engineering from Queen's University and her MS in aeronautical and astronautical engineering from Purdue University. Arly is funded through the Bilsland Dissertation Fellowship. In her spare time, she enjoys traveling, skiing, cycling, flying, reading, and hiking with her dog.
